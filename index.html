About Us

Phx Auto Care is a locally owned and operated business proudly serving the Phoenix community with top-quality mobile auto detailing services. We specialize in bringing the shine back to your vehicle with a personalized touch, offering expert cleaning and detailing tailored to meet the unique needs of your car, truck, or SUV. Whether you drive a daily commuter or a luxury ride, our team is committed to delivering exceptional results with care and precision. As a Phoenix-based company, we understand the impact of the desert climate on your vehicle and use high-quality products and proven techniques to protect and restore your car’s appearance. With flexible mobile service, we come to you—at home, at work, or wherever it's most convenient—making it easier than ever to keep your vehicle looking its best.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver an exceptional detailing service that enhances the look and longevity of your vehicle. We aim to offer the highest quality cleaning and detailing while providing convenient, mobile services at competitive prices.
